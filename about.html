About Megan Eaton

Designer and Front-End Developer

Contact

Megan Eaton has recently made the bold decision to change careers from international education/NPO work, to that of web design, UX/UI design, and graphic design. She has only recently started on this journey, but is working hard to gain the experience she needs to do well in this field.

Megan grew up in Vermont and later left to gain her bachelor's in International Studies at Colorado State University. During this time she studied abroad for a year in Osaka, Japan. After graduation, she once again returned to Japan to teach English as a second language for about three years. She now has returned to Vermont but hopes this new career path will allow her to travel the world again.
